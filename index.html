Our Juniors

Your journey with us begins at the age of 11! We will provide a safe, friendly and enjoyable environment where you will develop confidence both in terms of your basketball skill, but also in yourself. We have a space for everyone, regardless of your past experience. So, what are you waiting for?

Our Seniors

Train with a a diverse, hard-working, and committed group of athletes from all walks of life. Everyone in our senior group brings something different to the table, yet what unites us all is a love of the game! If you simply enjoy basketball and want a great place to play, then come on down and join in at a training session.

Our Accolades

It is fair to say that we have had incredible success over the years and, although Peterborough has become an increasingly competitive landscape, we remain a dominant force. We are proud to have been awarded the Peterborough League - Division 1 Winners trophy, 9 times since our inception. We know how to achieve success!
